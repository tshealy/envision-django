An example project - South LA Wetlands

Here is an example project to help with your assessment. The South Los Angeles Wetland Park is a nature park and sustainable stormwater treatment wetland constructed on a former Brownfield site in Los Angeles, CA. Both the previous site and end result are similar to what is possible in Cordova, AL. Included below are examples to explain how the South LA team achieved a platinum rating.

{#NW2.3#}

South Wetlands achieved Restorative.

The project team designed for 100% collection of dry weather runoff from the regional storm drain network. In addition, all wetland cells are lined with a primary geosynthetic clay liner (GCL) to form an impermeable layer that 1) prevents water loss via infiltration and 2) prevents infiltration into the former Brownfield site.
